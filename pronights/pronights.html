Terms and conditions
Pronight passes are free and complimentary with the Pravega'16 general registration.
Only one set of 3 passes will be issued for every unique registration.
Tickets/passes are not to be sold separately. No exceptions will be allowed.

Participants must comply with the security and Pravega'16 volunteers.

All final decisions rest with the Pravega'16 organizing team.
