ABOUT PRAVEGA

An IISc community effort, Pravega is a three-day fest that is an expression of our love for science, engineering, and the arts; a fiesta, truly, of the geek, by the geek, and for the geek. Endless fun, reams of learning, and the experience of a lifetime guaranteed!
