WHO
This hackathon will suit students with a background in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, Statistics, Analytics and Math. This event is open IISc students and attendees of Pravega 2016.
TEAMS & PRIZES
Hackthon participants will form teams (typically 3-4 students) to tackle the case study and dataset during the hackathon. At the end of the event, top teams will have the oppotuniry to present their work and analytical models to determine the winning teams. To be eligible for a prize, teams must demonstrate the use of Microsoft Azure ML in their solution. With cash prizes and give aways, it’s a great opportunity to build your skills, meet other top students and have fun.
