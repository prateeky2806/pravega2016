PRAVEGA 2016 | Indian Institute of Science
Description:
It’s time to show your airmanship by flying your innovative ideas in the field of aerospace. Present us with your idea and if it’s out of the box as well as implementable then you’ll be assigned a professor as mentor. Selected entrants will get an opportunity to present their ideas in finals during Pravega 2016.
Registrations: October 17, 2015 onwards

Prizes:
● Winning team – Prizes worth 20K (each member)
● Runner-up team- Prizes worth 10K (each member)
● Possible internship at Airbus.
● Possible funding by Airbus for further research into the idea.
Contact: Prayut Jain (+91-9972398178)

Rules:
1. Participants only from IISc Bangalore.
2. Team of 3 to 5 members.
3. There will be a preliminary round (i.e. the initial abstract provided at the time of registration).
Top few teams from prelims shall qualify for the finals.
4. Entries any later than 10th Nov, 11:59 A.M. won’t be considered.
5. Teams may submit ONE entry only. Submission of multiple entries shall lead to disqualification.
6. A participant can’t take part in the event from multiple teams.
7. Plagiarism will not be tolerated and shall lead to disqualification.
8. Judging criteria: Idea should be innovative and implementable.
9. The top two teams would be given prizes.
10. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding. In case of any disputes the organizing team of Pravega reserves the rights in its sole discretion.

Competition Timeline:
1. October 17, 2015: Registrations start.
2. November 10, 2015: Last date for registration and submission of a short title and brief abstract for your idea.
3. November 15, 2015: Announcement of prelim results. The selected teams will be assigned a professor.
4. January 20, 2016: Last date to confirm availability for the final event and to provide the soft copy of your presentation that would be presented by the team during Pravega 2016.
5. January 29-31, 2016: Finals of the Airbus Idea Presentation event.
