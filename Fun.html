Walk On Water

Yes! No lie… Swear, we will make you walk on water, and it will be fun. Just how exciting is the idea itself, isn’t it? We promise the unexpectedness of this. Wouldn’t you want to try it out?

LASER Tag

Settle all scores, let your livid intentions wild and time to remind people - who rules the jungle. This is laser tag! With the superb efforts of engineering team, we present to you a team strategy game where we will give you lasers and people to aim at. Let us see how many you kill.

Laser Maze

“My name is Bond, James Bond” Bring alive all your science fiction fantasies, Drift through the methodically placed lasers like a spy to get hold the prize before other do. You’ll have to find your way, it is not easy… you will have to bend in unusual ways, crawl like a snake, move like a fox, and show off like a pop star. Show how much you know yourself and who knows – a surreptitious underground organization may recruit you for your amazing skills.

MolecularMurals

This is one place where all the artistic and romantic souls would love to come. Let you submerge in the mysterious quest in the world of molecules and come back with flying colours, literally! Yes, create your own colours and paint your way into the heavenly hue of childhood memories, creating, in the process, indelible strokes on your mind that will be left on your mind for years to come.
